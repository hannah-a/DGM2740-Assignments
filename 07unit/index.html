FAQ

Should I tell my lawyer everything?

Yes, but don't spill the beans before you sign the contract. Your lawyer might be batting for the opposite team.

FAQ

I'm in jail, will you represent me on the spot?

No, you should've been a responsible adult and obtained a lawyer before getting in trouble.

You, your lawyer and the prosecutor showing up.
